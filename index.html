How to Play

  • You will receive a math problem to solve.
  • Type your answer in the input box and click "Submit".
  • If your answer is correct, you will gain points.
  • Note that your answer should always be rounded to the nearest whole number.
  • If your answer is incorrect, you might face consequences.
  • The game ends when you die.
  • Have fun!
